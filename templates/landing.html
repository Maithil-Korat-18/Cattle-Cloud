Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Cattle Cloud.

Can I track individual cattle or just the whole herd?
+

You can track each animal individually! Every cow gets a unique ID where you can record milk production, health history, breeding dates, and more. You can also view herd-level statistics to see overall performance trends.

Does the app work without internet connection?
+

Yes! Our mobile app has full offline functionality. You can record milk yields, health observations, and expenses even without internet. All data automatically syncs when you're back online, so nothing gets lost.

How much time does it take to log daily data?
+

Most farmers spend just 5-10 minutes per day logging milk production and health checks. Our quick-entry interface is designed for speed - just tap the cow ID and enter numbers. Many users actually save time compared to paper records.

Can multiple people access the same farm account?
+

Absolutely! You can add farm workers, family members, or managers with different permission levels. Everyone sees real-time updates, making teamwork much easier. Great for larger operations or when you're traveling.

What if I need help setting things up?
+

We provide free onboarding support for all new users! Our team will help you set up your account, add your cattle, and learn the features via video call or phone. We also have Hindi, Gujarati, and Marathi support available.
