Frequently Asked Questions

What is CattleTrack Pro? +
CattleTrack Pro is a comprehensive cattle management system designed to help farmers and ranchers efficiently manage their livestock operations. It includes modules for tracking milk production, monitoring animal health, managing feed schedules, and maintaining breed information.
How does milk yield tracking work? +
Our milk yield tracking module allows you to record daily production for each animal. The system automatically calculates weekly and monthly averages, identifies trends, and generates visual reports to help you optimize production.
Can I track health records for individual cattle? +
Yes! Each animal has a detailed health profile where you can log vaccinations, treatments, checkups, and any health issues. The system sends reminders for upcoming vaccinations and scheduled checkups.
Is the system suitable for small farms? +
Absolutely! CattleTrack Pro is designed to scale with your operation. Whether you have 5 cattle or 500, our system adapts to your needs. Small farms benefit from simplified management, while larger operations enjoy advanced analytics and reporting features.
How do I get started? +
