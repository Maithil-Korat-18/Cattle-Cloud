Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add and manage my cattle records?

expand_more
You can add individual animals through the "Add Cattle" form on your dashboard. Each record stores breed, date of birth, weight history, health notes, and milk data. You can also bulk import existing records using our CSV template.

Can I track milk production for individual cows?

expand_more
Absolutely. Cattle Cloud lets you log morning and evening yields per cow, track fat and protein percentages, and view weekly and monthly trend charts. Alerts notify you when a cow's production drops below your set threshold.

How does the health monitoring and alert system work?

expand_more
Our system monitors behavioral and production patterns and flags anomalies automatically. You receive email or dashboard alerts when unusual patterns are detected. You can also log vaccinations, treatments, and vet visits against each animal's profile.

Can I track feed and veterinary expenses per animal?

expand_more
Yes. You can allocate costs — feed, medicine, vet visits, supplements — to individual animals or groups. This gives you a clear picture of the cost-per-litre of milk and overall profitability per head.

How do I import my existing herd data?

expand_more
We offer a simple CSV import tool under Settings → Import Data. Download our template, fill in your herd details, and upload it. Our team can also assist with migration from other farm management software.

Is my farm data secure and private?

expand_more
Your data is encrypted at rest and in transit using industry-standard TLS and AES-256. We never share or sell your farm data. You can export or delete your data at any time from account settings.
